Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Return Tracker ("we" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address and password (or Google/Apple Sign-In credentials)
  • Return details: product names, stores, prices, dates, notes, and photos
  • Receipt information and images
  • Subscription tracking data
  • Optional: display name and profile picture

Automatically collected:

  • Device information and app usage (when analytics is enabled)
  • Location data (only when you use the "Find Nearby Stores" feature)
  • Error logs (when crash reporting is enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Return Tracker service and sync your data across devices
  • Send deadline reminder notifications and weekly digest emails
  • Process receipt images using AI to extract information
  • Show nearby store locations when requested
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve the app and fix bugs

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted cloud databases. Images are stored in encrypted cloud storage. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Cloud database and authentication providers
  • AI services for receipt scanning
  • Payment processors for subscriptions (Apple/Google)
  • Mapping services for store locations
  • Email delivery services
  • Error tracking services (optional, can be disabled)

We do NOT sell your data to third parties or share it with advertisers.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • View and update your data at any time in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data (permanent, cannot be undone)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics and notifications

7. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed immediately.

8. Children's Privacy

Return Tracker is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in different countries. We comply with GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California) requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
